Foundation for Affordable Education

Who we are

The Foundation for Affordable Education empowers children and schools in disadvantaged communities to receive necessary funding, training, tutoring, stationery, food and equipment to equip learners to matriculate in mathematics and science. We are a non-profit organization established in 2013 with currently branches operating in three provinces; Gauteng, Western Cape and Kwa-Zulu Natal. Our foundation has partnered with fifteen schools throughout these provinces to identify learners in need of financial, tutoring or social support.

Mission

To provide learners from disadvantaged schools affordable education, learning tools and supplies that aid in learning.

Projects

  • Funding full or partial school fees for learners in need
  • Ongoing tutoring to learners from Grade 0 - 12, in mathematics and science
  • Annual stationery drive (26 th April)
  • Distribution of food vouchers for families in need (provided by The Hope Foundation www.hope.org)
  • Distribution of 2 nd hand uniform, equipment, and textbook donations (as provided)
